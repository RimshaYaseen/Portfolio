Academic & Professional Training

A comprehensive overview of my formal university coursework alongside independent technical training and extra-curricular skill development.

Formal Degree Coursework (MPhil & MSc)

Theoretical foundations, advanced physiological studies, and research methodologies officially completed as part of my university syllabi.

Advanced Physiology

MPhil Level | Grade: A

Provided an in-depth analysis of the molecular and systemic mechanisms governing homeostasis. Key areas of study included neurophysiology (ion channel kinetics and synaptic transmission), cardiovascular dynamics (cardiac cycle and ECG interpretation), and the integrated functional roles of the renal and hepatic systems in metabolic regulation.

Hematology

MPhil Level | Grade: B+

This module focused on the morphology, kinetics, and pathophysiology of cellular blood components. It covered the molecular mechanisms of hematopoiesis, the functional roles of erythrocytes, leukocytes, and thrombocytes, and the biochemical cascades of hemostasis. Additionally, the study integrated the diagnostic analysis of hematological disorders.

Proteomics

MPhil Level | Grade: A

Examined the large-scale study of proteomes, focusing on protein structure, function, and expression. It covered high-throughput analytical techniques, including Mass Spectrometry (MS), SDS-PAGE, and 2D-Gel Electrophoresis. Key topics included post-translational modifications, protein-protein interactions, and the application of bioinformatics to map complex signaling pathways and identify functional biomarkers.

Environmental Sciences

MPhil Level | Grade: A+

Detailed exploration of ecosystem dynamics, biogeochemical cycling, and environmental toxicology. This course provided a comprehensive understanding of biodiversity conservation and the physiological impact of anthropogenic stressors. Through this curriculum, I mastered the analytical frameworks required to assess ecological health and mitigate environmental pollutants.

Basic & Applied Biostatistics

MSc & MPhil Level | Grade: A

Detailed exploration of experimental design and hypothesis testing within biological research. This course provided a comprehensive understanding of Two-Way ANOVA, Mixed Models, and regression analysis. Through this curriculum, I mastered the application of SPSS to ensure statistical rigor in animal & human physiology datasets.

Cell & Molecular Biology

MSc Level | Grade: A+

Detailed exploration of cellular architecture and molecular signaling pathways. This course provided a comprehensive understanding of DNA replication, transcription, translation, and the regulation of gene expression. Through this curriculum, I mastered the fundamental mechanisms of signal transduction, the cell cycle, and the molecular basis of inheritance and cellular differentiation.

Microbiology

MSc Level | Grade: A+

In-depth analysis of microbial taxonomy, physiology, and pathogenesis. This curriculum offered a rigorous study of bacterial genetics, virology, and immunology. Through these modules, I refined my expertise in aseptic techniques and metabolic pathways, focusing on their roles in infectious disease and biotechnology.

Applied Entomology

MSc Level | Grade: A

Advanced synthesis of insect biology, ecological impact, and pest management strategies. This curriculum provided a foundational mastery of Integrated Pest Management (IPM), biological control, and the physiological mechanisms of insecticide resistance. Through these modules, I cultivated specialized proficiency in taxonomic identification and the strategic application of entomological principles to agricultural and public health challenges.
